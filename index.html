About Trades Up Chelsea

We're excited to bring the Trades Up experience to Chelsea, Michigan! Our mission is to inspire young people to explore rewarding careers in the skilled trades through hands-on workshops and meaningful connections with local professionals.

Trades Up Chelsea will create opportunities for students to discover their potential by working alongside experienced tradespeople, building real skills, and understanding the diverse pathways available in construction, manufacturing, and skilled crafts.

Our first event is planned for Summer 2026, and we're actively seeking supporters to help make this vision a reality.

Events

Summer 2026 SPARK Event

Chelsea, Michigan

Our inaugural event will bring together local skilled trades professionals, students, and community members for a day of hands-on learning and exploration at the Chelsea Summer Festival Kids Zone. Participants will have the opportunity to try various trades, learn from experts, and spark their curiosity.
