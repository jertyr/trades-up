About Trades Up Chelsea

We're bringing the Trades Up experience to Chelsea, Michigan.

Our mission is to inspire young people to explore rewarding careers in the skilled trades through hands-on workshops and meaningful connections with local professionals. Students will discover their potential by working alongside experienced tradespeople, building real skills, and learning about diverse pathways in construction, manufacturing, and skilled crafts.

Our first event is planned for Summer 2026, and we're actively seeking supporters to help make this vision a reality.
